Services

Art Advisory

Tailored strategies for collectors, galleries, institutions and brands.

01
Collector
Advisory

Bespoke guidance for individual and institutional collectors. We help build meaningful, strategic collections — from emerging talents to blue-chip works. Our service includes market entry planning, acquisition strategy, portfolio diversification, and legacy consulting.

02
Gallery & Artist
Advisory

Strategic support for artists, galleries, and estates looking to grow internationally. From positioning and branding to fair participation and partnership building, we provide targeted insights and actionable plans tailored to your career stage or gallery goals.

03
Institutional &
Philanthropic Advisory

Custom strategies for museums, foundations, and public initiatives. We consult on curatorial direction, acquisitions, program development, and philanthropic art giving — ensuring long-term cultural impact through thoughtful planning and trusted networks.

04
Brand & Cultural
Partnerships

Creating meaningful collaborations between brands and the art world. We develop and curate culturally impactful projects — through immersive events, brand activations, artist commissions, or long-term cultural initiatives. Each project is tailored to align with both brand identity and artistic integrity.

Global Network

At KALDI ART, we offer bespoke advisory services rooted in deep market insight and a global network. Our extensive reach spans over 15 countries and 30+ cities, encompassing galleries, institutions, art professionals, collectors, and artists in a collaborative space. Whether you're building a collection, seeking strategic exposure, or exploring international expansion, our guidance is always discreet, research-driven, and forward-thinking.

The Practice

About

Shaping the Future of Contemporary Art — From Seoul to the World.

KALDI ART is a global art advisory operating between Asia and Europe, delivering active market expertise and strategic insight to collectors, galleries, and institutions worldwide.

We specialize in identifying exceptional works and facilitating high-level transactions, providing collectors with discreet, informed guidance and access to emerging and established artists across the international art market.

With over two decades of combined advisory experience, KALDI ART navigates the global art landscape with precision, drawing on a deeply trusted network of collectors, galleries, and institutions to connect clients to the opportunities and artworks that define contemporary collecting.

Our work is guided by a strong curatorial perspective, allowing us to recognize potential early and introduce compelling voices to the market with confidence. We combine comprehensive market insights, strategic vision, and global reach to ensure collectors engage with the art that shapes today's cultural landscape.
